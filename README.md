Walmart Sales Dashboard
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Walmart Sales Dashboard developed using Power BI. It provides comprehensive insights into sales performance across Walmart stores by analyzing weekly sales, seasonal patterns, store-level performance, and external economic factors.

The dashboard helps identify sales trends, evaluate store performance, and understand how external variables such as holidays, temperature, and unemployment influence business outcomes.

Business Problem

Retail businesses need to continuously monitor sales performance across multiple locations while understanding the impact of seasonal and economic factors. This dashboard addresses key business questions related to sales trends, store performance, and external influences.

Objectives
Analyze weekly sales performance across all Walmart stores.
Identify top-performing and underperforming stores.
Evaluate seasonal sales trends.
Assess the impact of holidays on sales.
Explore the relationship between sales and external factors such as temperature and unemployment.
